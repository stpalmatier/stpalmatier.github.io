Rockport, Massachusetts

Located at the tip of the Cape Ann peninsula, Rockport was originally settled

as Sandy Bay in the 1690s. The small settlement, which had previously been a source

of timber, by the early nineteenth century began quarrying operations and became known

as a granite exporter on the east coast.
